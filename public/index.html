Photographer Introduction

Enlarge

Ashley Wang is a 22-year-old young photographer. She loves traveling around the world. In 2012, when she visited an old friend in New York, she found out people might need a professional photographer traveling with them to take pictures for them. She thought it might be a good idea to start a related business. Since she was still in college at that time, she decided to wait. In 2016, she gruaduated with a Computer Science degree from University of Illinois. Even though she studies engineering, she is a big fan of fashion design and arts. After her gruadtion, she found a job as a part-time front-end engineer and started VineCharcoal Travel Photo Studio. Though she is busy for most of the times, she loves reading and cooking when she is free.

Works

Ashley took those pictures during travelling or just in daily life. These pictures are from all around the world such as Vancouver, Toronto, Guanajuato, Mexico, Nevada, Chicago, Seattle, California and so on.

She believes that each picture should have its own story and a good capture should deliver an overall feeling of the story to people. The beauty of a scene can be kept in either a good picture or memories.

Services & Fees

Services

Travel with the clients and take picutres for them any time in need based on their requirements. During the travel, Ashley will follow the clients' schedule and work with the clients all day just like a personal tour guide. She can keep taking the pictures until the clients are satisfied with the effect. Ashley can also offer her suggestions on clients' gestures for a better photo if needed.

She will be as professional as possible, but she keeps the right to refuse the clients' certain requirements if they are too dangerous.

Fees

No money need to pay directly to Ashley. But the client should cover her plane tickets, hotel fees, meals and tickets for tourism for her service. Client need to check Ashley's availability and confirm the schedule with her in advance. Plane tickets under her name should be provided as a booking confirmation of service. More details could be discussed with Ashley personally.
